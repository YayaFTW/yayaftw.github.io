I love you so much Hero  
Amidst the crowded loud café silence reigned,
For a second so brief yet so eternal it pained

My other half grew weary of my hollow words and vows,
My godsent grew jaded from my incessant whens and hows

Aïcha is unfailingly right, that day was no oddity,
I wish I was as sage, I covet her wisdom and clarity

For she was right. My whens and hows were but a mere excuse,
No reason was proper, No intention I had to confuse

A customary man is but a fruit of many flavors and stripes,
And through time, just as the man grows, the fruit ripes

A love red cherry, is savored tender before the essence festers,
As should a prime man act his words before their worth falters

I itch for this poem to reach your affection and kindness,
For I refuse to settle this matter with a taste of bitterness

I would convey you, queen of my soul to yield me one last chance,
That would be of no use, not a soul could hinder our romance

Thus I instead become the all luscious cherry of your liking,
A cherry you can relish, that no other would dream of taking

Know that my thoughtless actions are regretted and dearly,
And the way I was impetuous to you troubles my sleep nightly

For my love for you is a warm, graceful and respectful love,
A love that I pray day and night to be blessed from above

I love you in and out and from head to toe till we die old,
You are the angel I'd ever embrace, the hand I'd ever hold.
My eyes were blind, my words were not kind,
I was unable to soothe Aïcha's uneasy mind

From the fear that looms behind the future's mystery,
From the lessons of the past, and their aching misery

My temper, from my will and reason it stands apart,
And thus I could not nurse my hero's soft and frail heart

But now that my soul is serene,that my thoughts are bright,
Through this poem I wish to tell you, Aïcha my light

That our mesmerizing days, our future lies within,
The roots of the seeds we plant and believe in

With a heart beating hard and eyes staring in awe,
With eternal true love, a love that has no flaw

As my hand moans from the warmth of your skin,
As your beauty and purity melt me from within

At the sight of your eyes, those pearls I adore,
Your trust, your will and your bravery I implore

Let us hand in hand build the dreams of our future at last,
Let my undying love for you veil the history of the past.
Move your mouse over and see how hard my heart beats for you.
